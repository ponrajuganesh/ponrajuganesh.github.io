Mileage (Distance travelled so far) as measure of car performance
Distance travelled
before getting
repaired
The mileage signifies the distance the car has travelled before it was brought in for repair. The average mileage is the ratio of the total distance the particular car has travelled to the number of times a car of same of model has been brought in for a repair
Metric Usage

This measure could help,

car buyers to analyze the reliable car models under every make

Manufactures to concentrate more on these models so that are more reliable in the future
